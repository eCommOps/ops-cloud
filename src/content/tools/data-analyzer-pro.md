## Overview

Data Analyzer Pro combines powerful analytics with intuitive visualizations to help you understand your data and make informed decisions.

## Core Capabilities

- **AI-Powered Insights**: Automatically discover trends and anomalies
- **Interactive Dashboards**: Create beautiful, responsive dashboards
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Process and visualize data as it arrives
- **Custom Reports**: Generate automated reports on schedule
- **Collaborative Features**: Share insights with your team

## Data Sources

Connect to multiple data sources including:
- Databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB)
- Cloud storage (S3, Google Cloud Storage)
- APIs and webhooks
- CSV/Excel files
- Streaming data sources

## Visualization Types

- Line and area charts
- Bar and column charts
- Pie and donut charts
- Heat maps
- Geographic maps
- Custom visualizations

## AI Features

### Anomaly Detection
Automatically identify unusual patterns in your data.

### Predictive Analytics
Forecast future trends based on historical data.

### Natural Language Queries
Ask questions in plain English and get instant visualizations.
