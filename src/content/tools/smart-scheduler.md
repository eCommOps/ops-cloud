## Overview

Smart Scheduler uses AI to optimize your calendar, find ideal meeting times, and help you make the most of your day.

## Features

- **Intelligent Time Finding**: Analyzes everyone's calendars to suggest optimal meeting times
- **Automated Scheduling**: Share your booking link for instant scheduling
- **Calendar Sync**: Works with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar
- **Time Zone Intelligence**: Automatically handles global team scheduling
- **Meeting Preparation**: AI-generated meeting summaries and agendas

## How It Works

1. Connect your calendar
2. Set your availability preferences
3. Share your booking link or invite attendees
4. Smart Scheduler finds the best time for everyone
5. Automatically sends invites and reminders

## Smart Features

### Buffer Time
Automatically add buffer time between meetings to prevent back-to-back scheduling.

### Focus Time Protection
Reserve blocks of time for deep work that can't be booked.

### Meeting Analytics
Track how much time you spend in meetings and identify optimization opportunities.

- Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams
- Slack for meeting notifications
